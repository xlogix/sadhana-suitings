Denim

Sadhana has a large range of indigo dyed denims from 4.50 oz. to 15.5 oz. per sq. yd. weights that can fulfill most of the demands of apparel buyers. Each quality is produced to give a number of washing effects under offer. Technical guidance to produce the desired wash effects is an optional offer. The range is available in 100% Cotton and Cotton/Polyester blends, in rigid as well as in stretch and various fancy and slub designs.
